About Us

The Smackdown eSports Community promotes PES gaming in our region. We organize tournaments, offer resources, and create a vibrant platform for gamers of all levels. Whether you're pro or new, we welcome you to join the revolution!

We are the Oldest Co-op Tournament Organizers in India. Our community is built on the passion for PES and the desire to create a space where gamers can thrive. We host regular tournaments and foster a supportive environment for all players.

Our mission is to foster a community where PES enthusiasts can connect, compete, and grow together. We believe in the power of gaming to bring people together, and we strive to create an inclusive environment for all.
