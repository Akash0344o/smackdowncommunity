SMACKDOWN eFootball INDIA SHOWDOWN: Roadmap Revealed for Crew & Co-op Tournaments

Exclusive Esports Event Brings more Competitive eFootball Action to India

Kalyani, India - [01.07.2025] -The Smackdown Community has officially announced its roadmap for upcoming tournaments, featuring a Crew Tournament (SMACKDOWN eFootball INDIA SHOWDOWN) followed by a Co-op Tournament (SMACKDOWN CO-OP TOUR S-4) in August 2025. With cash prizes, team-based battles, and more surprises on the way, the Indian eFootball community is in for an exciting competitive season.

SMACKDOWN eFootball INDIA SHOWDOWN: Crew Tournament – Team Up & Dominate!

Format & Rules

  • 5v5 Matchday Battles: Each team consists of 5 main players, 2 substitutes, and 1 reserve player.
  • Entry Fee: ₹105.00 per player (Team captain handles registration).
  • Prize Pool: ₹3,000.00 for the winning crew.
  • Tournament Structure: Group stages followed by knockout rounds.
  • Matchdays: Fixtures will be scheduled weekly, with standings updated on the official SMACKDOWN WEBSITE

SMACKDOWN CO-OP TOUR S-4: Co-op Tournament – Team Up & Conquer!

Following the Crew Tournament, the SMACKDOWN eFootball INDIA SHOWDOWN will host a Co-op Tournament in August 2025, where players team up in pairs (2v2) for intense matches.

How to Register

Interested players can register for the Crew Tournament by visiting the official registration link. The registration deadline is 15.07.2025.
